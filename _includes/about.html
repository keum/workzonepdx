BACKGROUND

We want to hear your stories and create a comprehensive map of the conditions that exist on the ground for people walking and biking.

PROBLEM

Currently, the City of Portland does not have a comprehensive policy to address how street closures affect people walking and biking, especially people with disabilities and accesibility challenges.

PROPOSAL

We believe there should be a policy that ensures that safe and accessible detours are provided, that adjacent projects are conducted in a way that provides continual access to the pedestrian and bicycle network and that projects are not closing down needed routes for exorbitant amounts of time.
