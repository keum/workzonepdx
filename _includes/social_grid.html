Media Stream

Snapshots of sidewalk and road closures around the city.

Choose Your Method

Twitter

  1. Shoot a new photo or video with the mobile app
  2. Before you leave the site, add your location. Choose 'use precise location' for best accuracy.
  3. Type your tweet with the hashtag #workzoneWTF. Include what you observed or how you felt.

Instagram

  1. Shoot a new photo or video with the mobile app
  2. Before you leave the site, add your location.
  3. Type your caption with the hashtag #workzoneWTF. Include what you observed or how you felt.

Extra Tips

  • Your posts must be public or we won't be able to see them!
  • If you cannot add a location or are not at the location, then type the nearest intersection or landmark into your tweet.
  • If you do not have a mobile phone then post to Twitter from your desktop and type the name of the nearest intersection or landmark into your post.
  • If you want to use Twitter or Instagram but don't want to post publicly on your account then direct message to @vzworkzones on Twitter or vzworkzones on Instragram and we'll repost anonymously.

Email

  • Send images or videos with description to vzworkzones@gmail.com. By submitting you agree to allow us to post them to Twitter on your behalf.

Other

  • If you have more to share then submit a story to us. Just go to the story section of this site and fill out the form.

Need Help?

  • Send your questions to vzworkzones@gmail.com and we will follow up.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What if my phone can't figure out my location when I try to add it? Then include the nearest address, intersection or landmark in the post text and we'll take care of it.
  • What if I forgot to add the location or mess up in some other way? Instagram will let you edit your post but Twitter will not and you will have to delete and repost. Your original image or video should still be accessible
